- The DOF defines connected_outputs as strings, but the SNMP OID outputAllInChNr likely expects an Integer.

The firmware behavior is the source of truth — NOT the MIB descriptions.
The hardware firmware is INPUT-INDEXED ONLY.
This applies to the following MIB OIDs:
* outInChNr
* outputAllInChNr
* outputAllOutChNr
* inOutChNr
* inInChNr
* inputAllOutChNr
Regardless of what the MIB says:
* index = INPUT PORT
* value = OUTPUT PORT
Example:outputAllInChNr.6 = 4, ACTUALLY means:input 6 → output 4, NOT:output 6 ← input 4
